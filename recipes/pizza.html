Ingredients

  • Pizza of your choice
  • Topping of your choice. We like mushrooms and black olives

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 500 degrees.
  2. Open up your pizza box.
  3. Toss or throw the pizza into the oven.
  4. Wait until you can almost smell a fire.
  5. Once it's completely black, it's ready to go!
  6. Now throw it away!
