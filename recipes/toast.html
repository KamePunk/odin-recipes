World's Best Toast!

Back To Main Page

Ingredients

  • Bread of your choice
  • Topping of your choice. We like butter here but peanut butter is also good!

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to high heat or preheat your oven to 400 degrees F or use a toaster.
  2. In a medium bowl, stir together the goat cheese, lemon juice and thyme.
  3. Place the bread on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil on both sides of each slice, season with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the bread on the grill and grill both for about 15 minutes per side or until extremely toasted.
  5. Wait until you can almost smell a fire.
  6. Once it's completely black, it's ready to go!
  7. Now throw it away!
