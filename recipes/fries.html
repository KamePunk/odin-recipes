Ingredients

  • French Fries of your choice
  • Topping of your choice. We like cheese and spicy ranch

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 500 degrees.
  2. Pour out the bag of fries onto a pan
  3. Even them out, or not.
  4. Wait until you can almost smell a fire.
  5. Once it's completely black, it's ready to go!
  6. Now throw it away!
